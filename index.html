Welcome

Welcome to the Resume Builder website. This project is designed to help students create a professional resume by entering their personal information, educational qualifications, technical skills, projects, certifications, and achievements.

A well-designed resume is one of the most important documents for every student. It creates the first impression during placement drives, internships, and job interviews.

Why Use This Resume Builder?

  • Create a professional resume easily.
  • Organize your educational details.
  • Add technical and soft skills.
  • Showcase academic projects.
  • Prepare for campus placements.
  • Keep all information in one place.

Features

Personal Information

Store your name, email, phone number, address, and social profiles.

Education Details

Add your school, college, degree, department, and CGPA.

Projects

Display your academic and personal projects with technologies used and project descriptions.

Resume Preview

View all your entered information in a resume format before printing or saving.

Steps to Build Your Resume

  1. Enter your personal details.
  2. Add educational qualifications.
  3. Provide your technical skills.
  4. Add projects and certifications.
  5. Preview your complete resume.

Resume Tips

"Your resume is your first opportunity to make a positive impression. Keep it simple, honest, and professional."
